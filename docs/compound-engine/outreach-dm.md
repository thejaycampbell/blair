# Blair Founder Outreach — DM Template

**Goal:** 15 DMs. 10 founders who post their Blair output publicly.
**Timeline:** Wave 1 (8 DMs) on sprint Day 3. Wave 2 (7 DMs) at day 10.

## Target Profile

Solo founders or small-team builders who:
- Post about building with AI IDEs (Cursor, Claude Code)
- Have 500-10,000 followers (smaller = faster to respond)
- Have a real product they are actively marketing

**Communities:** Cursor subreddit, Claude Discord, Indie Hackers, X/Twitter AI builder circles.

---

## The DM

> Hey [Name] --
>
> Saw your post about [specific thing they built or shared]. Good stuff.
>
> I built Blair -- an AI CMO agent that installs into Claude Code or Cursor. Brief it once on your brand. Every marketing ask after that gets a brand-specific answer instead of a generic one.
>
> One ask: run it on your brand for 10 minutes. Post what you got -- good, bad, or ugly.
>
> Install: `npx blair-cmo` (Claude Code) or `npx blair-cmo --ide cursor` (Cursor)
> Docs: github.com/thejaycampbell/blair
>
> If the output is useful, keep it. If not, you've spent 10 minutes and you owe me nothing.
>
> -- Jay
